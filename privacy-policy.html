Privacy Policy for MegaSim

Effective Date: June 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how William Shoiti Saito LTDA ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile game, MegaSim (the "Game").

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information Collected Automatically

When you play MegaSim, we or our third-party partners automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with the Game. This helps us display relevant ads and understand how the Game is used. This information includes:

  • Device identifiers: Such as your Android Advertising ID (AAID or GAID).
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, mobile network information, and screen size.
  • IP address: Used to approximate your general location and for fraud prevention.
  • Usage data: Information about your interactions with the Game, like the duration of your sessions, the ads you view and click on, and crash reports.

1.2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our third-party advertising partner, Google AdMob, may use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies. These technologies collect information about your device and your activity within MegaSim and across other apps and websites. This data helps AdMob deliver targeted advertisements and measure their effectiveness.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and provide you with the features and functionality of MegaSim.
  • To deliver and display advertisements within the Game.
  • To analyze and improve MegaSim's performance and user experience.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or other illegal activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • With Advertising Partners: We share device and usage information (like your Android Advertising ID and IP address) with our third-party advertising partner, Google AdMob. AdMob uses this information to:
    • Serve personalized advertisements to you.
    • Limit the number of times you see a particular ad.
    • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.
    • Perform ad reporting and analytics.

    For more information on how Google AdMob collects and processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy and Google's policies for AdMob at: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543.

  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).
  • To Protect Our Rights: We may disclose your information when we believe it's necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding potential violations of our terms of service, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of William Shoiti Saito LTDA, our users, or others.
  • In Connection with Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Aggregated or Anonymized Data: We may share aggregated or anonymized information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you, with third parties for various purposes, including analytics and marketing.

4. Data Retention

We retain the information we collect for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. AdMob's data retention policies are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising: You can generally limit personalized advertising by adjusting the privacy settings on your Android device:
    • Go to Settings > Google > Ads.
    • Tap "Reset advertising ID" to get a new identifier.
    • Tap "Delete advertising ID" to remove it completely.
    • You can also enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization."

    These actions may limit the ability of AdMob and other ad networks to serve personalized ads, but you may still see generic ads.

  • Other Rights: Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., if you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Brazil), you may have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information. Please contact us using the information below to inquire about exercising these rights.

6. Children's Privacy

MegaSim is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. While AdMob may show ads, you are responsible for correctly setting your target audience in the Google Play Console, which influences AdMob's behavior regarding child-directed apps. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information (beyond what AdMob might incidentally collect as part of its general ad serving), please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information from our systems.

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information, including data collected by AdMob, may be stored and processed in any country where we or Google (AdMob's operator) have facilities or where we engage service providers. By using MegaSim, you understand that your information may be transferred to countries outside of your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws than your country. As you are located in Brazil, and your users may be in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or other regions, we and Google take appropriate measures to ensure that your data is handled in accordance with GDPR, UK GDPR, and LGPD respectively, when transferred outside these regions, such as by using standard contractual clauses.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of MegaSim after any modifications to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the modified Privacy Policy.
